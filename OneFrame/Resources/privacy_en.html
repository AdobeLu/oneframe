Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the OneFrame (hereinafter "we") iOS application.

1. Information Collection and Use

We may collect the following information when you use this application:

  • Camera Permission: Used to capture photos and videos. We do not use your camera without notice. All captured content is stored locally on your device only.
  • Location Information: Only obtained when you enable the info watermark feature, used to display coordinates and city name in photo/video watermarks. We do not upload your location to any server or use it for other purposes.
  • Photo Library Permission: Used to save captured photos and videos to your system photo album.

2. Data Storage

All photos and videos taken with this app are stored locally on your device. We do not upload any of your content to any server, nor do we share it with third parties.

3. In-App Purchases

This app offers a Premium membership (one-time purchase) through Apple App Store In-App Purchases. All payment information is processed by Apple, and we cannot access your credit card or payment details.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Apple's In-App Purchase service (StoreKit). Your purchase records are managed by Apple.

5. Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for users of all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
